Understanding Autism Spectrum Disorder

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior. Early detection and intervention can significantly improve outcomes for children diagnosed with ASD.

Our mission is to provide accessible tools that help identify potential signs of ASD, empowering families to seek professional guidance at the earliest opportunity.

How Our Assessment Works

This website provides a preliminary autism assessment by analyzing basic details and responses to a short questionnaire. While this test is not a medical diagnosis, it can help identify potential risk factors that may warrant further professional evaluation.

The process is simple, confidential, and can be completed in just a few minutes from the comfort of your home.

What We Assess

The Q-CHAT-10 is a quantitative checklist and assessment completed by parents or guardians who have concerns about their toddler's risk of autism.

  • Child's basic information (name, age, gender)
  • Ethnic background (for research purposes)
  • Responses to 10 key autism-related behavioral questions
  • Social communication patterns
  • Repetitive behaviors and restricted interests

All information is treated with the utmost confidentiality and used solely for assessment purposes.
