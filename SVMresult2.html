The Q-CHAT (Quantitative Checklist for Autism in Toddlers) is a screening tool that helps identify children who may benefit from a more comprehensive evaluation for autism spectrum disorder (ASD). This is not a diagnosis, but rather an indicator of whether further assessment might be warranted.
